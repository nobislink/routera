Upgrading your plan ensures you have access to premium features and expanded assignment capacity.

### Upgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings** <Icon icon="gear" /> and open the **Billing** tab.
2. Click **Upgrade Now**.
3. Select the plan you want from the widget, then click **Upgrade**.

You’ll see the following payment prompt:

![Clean Shot2025 08 18at16 06 50 Pn](/images/CleanShot2025-08-18at16.06.50.png)

4. Under **Save payment information**, choose your payment method (**Card** or **Bank**) and enter your details.
5. (Optional) Check **Save my information for faster checkout** <Icon icon="check" />.
6. Click **Save Card/Bank** to confirm. This will charge your selected payment method.
7. If successful, you’ll receive a confirmation message, and your payment method will appear in the **Billing** section of your account.

![Clean Shot2025 08 18at16 23 32 Pn](/images/CleanShot2025-08-18at16.23.32.png)

### Changing Your Payment Method

To update your payment method:

1. Go to **Settings** <Icon icon="gear" />.
2. Under **Payment Method**, click **Manage**.
3. On the next page, click **\+ Add Payment Information**.
4. Select your payment method and fill in the required details.
5. Check <Icon icon="check" /> **Use as default payment method**, then click **Add**.
