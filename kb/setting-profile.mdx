- **Name:** Enter your first and last name.  
- **Country:** Select your country of residence.  
- **Timezone:** Choose your timezone by Continent/City. The abbreviation (e.g., EST) and UTC offset will also display.  
- **Your Photo:** Upload a profile picture in SVG, PNG, JPG, or GIF format (maximum size: 5MB).  
- **Bio:** Add a short introduction to your profile.  

<Note>
  Click **Save** to apply your changes.
