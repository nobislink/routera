To update a router:

1. Go to **Routers** in the left sidebar.
2. Click the router you want to update.

### Deleting a Router

1. In the **Routers** list, select the router by clicking its checkbox.
2. Click <Icon icon="trash-can" /> **Delete**.
3. Confirm the deletion.

![Clean Shot2025 08 18at11 00 08 Pn](/CleanShot2025-08-18at21.31.14.png)

<Note>
  Deleting a router cannot be undone
</Note>
