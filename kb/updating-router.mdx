---
title: "Updating a router"
description: "This article will guide you through the process of updating an existing router"
---

To update a router, go to **Routers** on the left sidebar, and then click on the router you want to update:

![Clean Shot2025 08 18at10 53 14 Pn](/images/CleanShot2025-08-18at10.53.14.png)

You'll see the following four tabs you saw when you [created a router](#). Modify your router sections accordingly.

If you want to **delete** a router, select it by clicking on its checkbox, and then on <Icon icon="trash-can" />**Delete** .

![Clean Shot2025 08 18at11 00 08 Pn](/images/CleanShot2025-08-18at11.00.08.png)
