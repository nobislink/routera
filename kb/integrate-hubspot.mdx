If youâ€™re using HubSpot CRM, you can integrate your account with Routera. To connect your HubSpot account:

1. Go to **Settings** <Icon icon="gear" /> and open the **Integrations** tab.  
2. Under **HubSpot**, click **Connect**.  
3. Select the HubSpot account you want to connect, then click **Choose Account**.  

![Clean Shot2025 08 15at15 47 55 Pn](/images/CleanShot2025-08-15at15.47.55.png)

4. Review the permissions requested on the next screen, then click **Connect App**.  
5. Once connected successfully, your HubSpot account will appear in the **Integrations** section of Routera.
