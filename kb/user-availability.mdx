### Checking your own's user availability

In order to set your own user's availability, open your **Settings** <Icon icon="gear" /> on the upper right side of your screen, then click on **Working Hours. **You'll see the following sections on the screen:

### Availability

This switch will allow you to manually turn your availability on or of, regardless of the working hours you've set on **Working Hours**

### Timezone

This is your location's timezone. The **Working Hours** you select below will be based within this time zone.

### Working hours

This would be the schedule Routera will use as a basis for your availlability and receive assignments. Assignments will occur within these hours as long as your **Availability **is turned on.
