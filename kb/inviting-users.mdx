---
title: "Inviting Users to Your Workspace"
description: "This article will guide you through the process of inviting users to your workspace."
---

After logging in to your workspace, go to **Settings** <Icon icon="gear" /> (located in the upper right section of the screen) and open the **User** tab, and click **Add User**:

![Screenshot2025 08 13at5 00 23PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-13at5.00.23PM.png)

Fill in the required information in the sidebar on the right side of the screen, then click **Create User**:

![Screenshot2025 08 13at5 07 43PM Pn](/CleanShot2025-08-18at21.25.20.png)

<Note>
  You can assign one of two roles to a new user: **Administrator** or **User**. **Administrators** have full access to your workspace, whereas **Users** can only access their profile preferences.
</Note>
