---
title: "Inviting Users to Your Workspace"
description: "This article will guide you through the process of inviting users to your workspace."
---

After logging in to your workspace, go to **Settings** (located in the upper right section of the screen):

![Screenshot2025 08 13at4 56 54PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-13at4.56.54PM.png)

Open the **User** tab:

![Screenshot2025 08 13at4 59 11PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-13at4.59.11PM.png)

Click **Add User**:

![Screenshot2025 08 13at5 00 23PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-13at5.00.23PM.png)

Fill in the required information in the sidebar on the right side of the screen, then click **Create User**:

![Screenshot2025 08 13at5 07 43PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-13at5.07.43PM.png)

<Note>
  You can assign one of two roles to a new user: **Administrator** or **User**. **Administrators** have full access to your workspace, whereas **Users** can only access their own profile preferences. More info here.
</Note>

![Screenshot2025 08 13at5 36 48PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-13at5.36.48PM.png)

✅ **You’ve successfully added a user to your workspace.**
