### Creating Teams

To create a **Team** on Routera, go to **Settings** <Icon icon="gear" /> on the upper right corner of your screen, then click on **Teams \> Create team**

![Clean Shot2025 08 14at11 15 46 Pn](/images/CleanShot2025-08-14at11.15.46.png)

A _sidebar_ will appear on the right side of your screen with the following sections:

- **Team name:** The name for your team (example: Marketing)
- **Team managers**: The team managers are users that will be able to modify and manage team members **Availability** and **Working Hours**
- **Team members:** The team members are users who will be receiving assignments

To proceed, select team members and team managers for your team as depicted below, and then click on **Create Team** when finished.

### Modifying a team

To modify a **Team** on Routera, go to **Settings** <Icon icon="gear" /> on the upper right corner of your screen, then click on **Teams**. Click on Team's name you want to modify

![Clean Shot2025 08 14at11 38 49 Pn](/images/CleanShot2025-08-14at11.38.49.png)

To add a **Manager** to your team, click on **Add Managers**. A sidebar will appear on the right side of the screen, where you can select users to be added as Managers. When you're done, click on **Add**

![Clean Shot2025 08 14at11 45 49 Pn](/images/CleanShot2025-08-14at11.45.49.png)

To add a **Memeber** to your team, click on the **Members** tab, and then click on **Add Users**. Then repeat the same process.

When you're done, click on  <Icon icon="arrow-left" /> **Back to all teams**

![Clean Shot2025 08 14at11 59 23 Pn](/images/CleanShot2025-08-14at11.59.23.png)
