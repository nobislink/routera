### Creating Teams

To create a **Team** on Routera, go to **Settings** <Icon icon="gear" /> in the upper-right corner of your screen, then click on **Teams \> Create Team**.

![Clean Shot2025 08 14at11 15 46 Pn](/images/CleanShot2025-08-14at11.15.46.png)

A sidebar will appear on the right side of your screen with the following sections:

- **Team name:** The name for your team (example: Marketing)
- **Team managers:** Users who can modify and manage team members’ **Availability** and **Working Hours**
- **Team members:** Users who will be receiving assignments

To proceed, select team members and team managers for your team as depicted below, and then click **Create Team** when finished.

![Clean Shot2025 08 14at11 28 57 Pn](/images/CleanShot2025-08-14at11.28.57.png)

<Check>
  **You’ve now created your first team.**
</Check>

### Modifying a Team

To modify a **Team** on Routera, go to **Settings** <Icon icon="gear" /> in the upper-right corner of your screen, then click on **Teams**. Click on the team’s name you want to modify.

![Clean Shot2025 08 14at15 53 58 Pn](/images/CleanShot2025-08-14at15.53.58.png)

To add a **Manager** to your team, click **Add Managers**. A sidebar will appear on the right side of the screen, where you can select users to be added as managers. When you're done, click **Add**.

To add a **Member** to your team, click the **Members** tab, then click **Add Users**. Repeat the same process.

<Note>
  Adding a current Team Member as a Manager will remove them from the Team Member section and vice versa
</Note>

When you're done, click <Icon icon="arrow-left" /> **Back to all teams**.

<Check>
  **You have successfully modified your team.**
</Check>

### Deleting a team

To delete a **Team** on Routera, go to **Settings** <Icon icon="gear" /> in the upper-right corner of your screen, then click on **Teams**. Select the team you want to delete, and then click on **Delete** <Icon icon="trash-can" />

![Clean Shot2025 08 14at15 56 42 Pn](/images/CleanShot2025-08-14at15.56.42.png)

<Check>
  **You have successfully deleted your team.**
</Check>
