## Get started in three steps

Get your first router started in minutes with these three steps

### Step 1: Sign Up for Routera

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Sign Up for Routera" icon="star">
    Create your Routera account with the steps mentioned [in this article](/kb/signing-up)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Connect your HubSpot account

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Connect your HubSpot account" icon="hubspot">
    Although it is not required, we strongly recommend you do this. [Follow these instructions](/kb/integrate-hubspot) to connect your HubSpot account to Routera
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Create a router and set it up in HubSpot

<Accordion title="Set up your router" icon="rocket">
  Follow [these instructions](/kb/creating-routers) to create a router in Routera and set it up in HubSpot
</Accordion>
