---
title: "Inviting users to your workspace"
description: "This article will guide you through the process of inviting users thorough workspace"
---

After logging in to your workspace, go to **Settings** (the on the upper left section of the screen:

![Screenshot2025 08 13at4 56 54PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-13at4.56.54PM.png)

Open the **User** tab:

![Screenshot2025 08 13at4 59 11PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-13at4.59.11PM.png)

Click on **Add User**

![Screenshot2025 08 13at5 00 23PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-13at5.00.23PM.png)

Provide the information requested on the sidebar shown on the right side of the screen, and then click **Create User**:

![Screenshot2025 08 13at5 07 43PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-13at5.07.43PM.png)

<Note>
  You'll notice you can add two roles to a new user: **Administrator** and **User**. **Administrators** will have full access to your workspace, whereas **Users** can only access their profile preferences. More info here.
</Note>
