## Creating an account:

When you open the page https://app.v2.routera.io/login, you'll see the following prompt:

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot2025-08-12at4.22.53PM.png"
  alt="login_prompt"
  title="Screenshot2025 08 12at4 22 53PM Pn"
  style={{ width:"100%" }}
/>

Create an account by clicking "Create Acount"

![Screenshot2025 08 12at4 34 30PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-12at4.34.30PM.png)

Enter your email address; alternatively, click on "Sign up with Google" if you have a Google or Gmail account:

![Screenshot2025 08 12at4 43 09PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-12at4.43.09PM.png)

If you entered your email address, you'll receive a password-free sign-up link on your email similar to the one below:

![Screenshot2025 08 12at4 48 31PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-12at4.48.31PM.png)

<Tip>
  If you're unable to find this email after a couple of minutes, check your email's Spam folder
</Tip>

When clicking "Log in to Routera" or copying and pasting the link below that button to your browser, you'll see the following screen. Answer the question on screen and click "Continue"

![Screenshot2025 08 12at5 02 42PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-12at5.02.42PM.png)

Answer the questions shown on the screen, and then click "Next":

![Screenshot2025 08 12at5 16 11PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-12at5.16.11PM.png)

On the following screen, you'll be asked to add your team members. You can add multiple users by adding their emails, separated by commas.

![Screenshot2025 08 12at5 21 21PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-12at5.21.21PM.png)

<Note>
  You may skip this step by clicking on "Not Now" and add those users later
</Note>
