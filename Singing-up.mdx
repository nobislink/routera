---

### 1. Go to the Login Page 

Open https://app.v2.routera.io/login. \
You’ll see the login prompt:

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot2025-08-12at4.22.53PM.png"
  alt="login_prompt"
  title="Screenshot2025 08 12at4 22 53PM Pn"
  style={{ width:"100%" }}
/>

### 2. Create Your Account 

Click **Create Account**.

![Screenshot2025 08 12at4 34 30PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-12at4.34.30PM.png)

### 3. Choose Your Sign-Up Method 

You can:

- **Enter your email address** and receive a password-free login link, **or**
- **Sign up with Google** if you have a Google or Gmail account.

![Screenshot2025 08 12at4 43 09PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-12at4.43.09PM.png)

### 4. Confirm Your Email (If Using Email Sign-Up) 

If you entered your email address, check your inbox for a password-free sign-up link like the one below:

![Screenshot2025 08 12at4 48 31PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-12at4.48.31PM.png)

<Tip>
  If you're unable to find this email after a couple of minutes, check your email's Spam folder
</Tip>

### 5. Complete Onboarding Questions 

You’ll be taken to an onboarding screen. Answer the questions displayed and click **Continue** to proceed.

![Screenshot2025 08 12at5 02 42PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-12at5.02.42PM.png)

![Screenshot2025 08 12at5 16 11PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-12at5.16.11PM.png)

### 6. Add Your Team Members (Optional) 

On the next screen, you can invite team members by entering their email addresses, separated by commas.

![Screenshot2025 08 12at5 21 21PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-12at5.21.21PM.png)

<Tip>
  If you prefer, click **Not Now** to skip adding team members. You can invite them later from the **Settings** section.
</Tip>

### 7. Access Your Workspace 

You’ll see your newly created Workspace. Click your company’s name to log in.

![Screenshot2025 08 12at5 28 30PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-12at5.28.30PM.png)

✅ **You’re now signed up and ready to start using Routera\!**
